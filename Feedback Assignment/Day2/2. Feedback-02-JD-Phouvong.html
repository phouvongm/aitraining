ໝວດ 8 (Decision Authority) ເປັນ JD ທີ່ລາຍລະອຽດດີທີ່ສຸດໃນ Batch: 11 ລາຍການ ລະບຸ threshold ຊັດ — ງົບ Operating ≤20 ລ້ານ/Approval, Vendor Contract ≤50 ລ້ານ, Cyber Incident 4 ຊົ່ວໂມງທຳອິດຕັດສິນໃຈໄດ້ເຕັມ. ໂຄງສ້າງນີ້ ຕອບຄຳຖາມ "ຮູ້ຈັກຂອບເຂດຂອງຕົນ" ໄດ້ທຸກກໍລະນີ
Section 8 Decision Authority is the most detailed in the batch: 11 items with clear thresholds — Operating budget ≤20M kip, Vendor contracts ≤50M, full authority in first 4 hours of a cyber incident. This structure answers "know your boundaries" for every scenario.
ໝວດ 7 (Working Relationships) ມີ 5 ກຸ່ມ ຄອບຄຸມທຸກມິຕິ: ພາຍໃນ (CEO, Risk, Operations, Finance, HR, Audit, Branches), ການກຳກັບ (BOL, Payment System, Tech Dept), ໂຄງການ (ADA, ICCAP, JITS), ສຖາບັນການເງິນ (BCEL, LDB), Telecom (ETL, LTC, Unitel), Vendors (AWS, Optimal, External Auditors) — ນີ້ຄືແຜນທີ່ Network ທີ່ Head of IT ຕ້ອງຮູ້
Section 7 Working Relationships spans 5 groups covering every dimension: internal leadership, BOL regulators, development partners (ADA/ICCAP), banking partners (BCEL/LDB), telecoms (ETL/LTC/Unitel), and vendors — a complete network map for the Head of IT role.
ສະເພາະດ້ານ IT ສູງ ແລະ ຖືກຕ້ອງກັບ EMI: ລວມທັງ MPLS, Leased lines, USSD services, Optimal Core Banking, AWS Cost Management, NIST CSF / ISO 27001, CAB (Change Advisory Board), SPI4 reporting ສຳລັບ ADA — ທ່ານຂຽນຈາກຄວາມຮູ້ຈິງ ບໍ່ໃຊ້ Template ທົ່ວໄປ
High domain specificity relevant to EMI: MPLS/leased lines for branch connectivity, USSD services, Optimal Core Banking, AWS Cost Management, NIST CSF, Change Advisory Board (CAB), and SPI4 reporting for ADA — written from genuine knowledge, not a generic IT template.
